Supply Chain Flow 1 — Company → Wholesaler
☐  Company adds a new medicine to catalogue
☐  Wholesaler sees it when searching medicines to buy
☐  Wholesaler requests to become supplier → company approves → wholesaler notified
☐  Company creates offer → wholesaler receives FCM notification
☐  Wholesaler accepts offer → company sees it moved to Accepted tab
☐  Wholesaler places bulk order → company sees it and approves
☐  Wholesaler confirms receipt → wholesaler inventory updates correctly (verify in Supabase)

Supply Chain Flow 2 — Wholesaler → Retailer
☐  Wholesaler creates scheme for retailer
☐  Retailer searches to restock → sees wholesaler with scheme applied automatically
☐  Retailer places purchase order → wholesaler receives FCM notification
☐  Wholesaler approves with custom pricing → retailer sees updated price
☐  Retailer pays → wholesaler sees payment confirmed
☐  Wholesaler dispatches → retailer receives FCM notification
☐  Retailer confirms receipt → retailer inventory updates (verify in Supabase)

Supply Chain Flow 3 — Retailer → Customer
☐  Retailer's inventory visible in customer search (verify medicine appears)
☐  Customer orders from retailer → retailer receives FCM notification
☐  Retailer approves prescription → customer receives FCM: approved
☐  Retailer dispatches → customer tracks in app, sees status update
☐  Customer confirms delivery → order closes → feedback prompt appears
☐  Retailer sees completed order in analytics

☐  Verify medicine-wise sales match actual orders in Supabase
